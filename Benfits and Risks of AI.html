Benfits and Risks of AI

Main Advantages of AI

  1. Less routine
  2. Less risk
  3. No brakes
  4. No human error
  5. No slowdown
  6. Fewer chores
  7. Better forecasts
  8. Better resources distribution
  9. Less disease

Main Disadvantages of AI

  1. Fewer workplaces
  2. Lack of ethics
  3. More sloth
